Teacher's Salary Calculator

Enter your gross salary as of September 2023 to calculate your net take-home pay and the effect of the 6.5% pay rise.

How the Calculator Works

The calculator considers the following factors to provide an estimate of your net take-home pay:

  • Student Loan Repayments: Deductions based on your selected student loan plan. Plan 1 and Plan 2 have different thresholds and repayment rates.
  • Pension Contributions: Calculated according to your annual salary. The rate of contribution varies depending on your salary band.
  • Tax: Income tax is calculated based on current UK tax bands and personal allowance thresholds.
  • National Insurance: Contributions are calculated on your gross salary above the tax-free allowance, with different rates applied to different salary bands.
