Teacher's Back-dated Pay Calculator

This calculator helps UK teachers estimate their back-dated pay due to a recent pay rise. It considers the following:

  • Student Loan Repayments: Deductions based on the selected student loan plan.
  • Pension Contributions: Calculated according to your annual salary and relevant pension contribution rates.
  • Net Payment: The final amount is your net back-dated pay after considering taxes, student loan repayments, and pension contributions.
